About
Epic

Embracing Big Data in Health

The potential for big data in health is endless. We built our company to empower researchers, physicians and healthcare providers to generate new insights, create new therapies, and better manage care for patients.

Our company is based on cutting-edge predictive models developed by MIT scientists and engineers. This data science is allowing us to unlock patterns in the massive amounts of health data that are being generated today.
