Common Questions

What sport injuries are covered?


Any sport you can think of is covered from school sports to action and extreme sports.


What if I get hurt more than once in a year?


Not to worry, our accident plan will fully cover each and every accident or injury.


Do I need to first have a major medical policy?


Epic plans will cover you no matter what.


If I've already paid my major medical deductible for the year and I get hurt will the Epic plan still benefit me?


Our plans are stand-alone and will pay you per accident regardless of your major medical policy status.


Is there a waiting period before I'm covered by your plan?


As soon as your plan is active you are covered.


If I file a claim will my rates increase?


Filing a claim will not affect your monthly rates.


What about my spouse and children, are they covered?


Epic offers family plans to cover the whole family.


Is there a payout limit for each accident?


Most plans will pay up to $2,500, however, there is no limit to how much the Epic plans may pay out.
